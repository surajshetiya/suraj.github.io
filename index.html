Short Bio

Suraj Shetiya is a PhD candidate at University of Texas at Arlington. He is pursuing his research under the guidance of Dr Gautam Das.

His research lies in the area of Data Management and Fairness. In addition, he is interested in algorithmic and conceptual problems in computational geometry, graph algorithms, and metric space, with the goal to design efficient algorithms.
